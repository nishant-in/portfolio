Hello! I'm Akansha Sharma!!
My journey into web development is rooted in a deep love for learning and problem-solving. Growing up in New Delhi, I was drawn to the sciences, studying C++ alongside my core subjects in school. This curiosity for logic and structure led me to pursue a Bachelor's in Elementary Education (B.El.Ed) with a specialization in Chemistry at Delhi University. Teaching became my first passion, allowing me to simplify complex ideas and share knowledge with others.
However, my fascination with technology and its power to create impactful solutions led me to explore coding beyond the classroom. Determined to turn my interest into expertise, I pursued a Front-End Development certification from iNeuron Institute. With each project, I found joy in bringing ideas to life through clean, user-friendly, and interactive web experiences.
Beyond coding, I believe in giving back to the community. Teaching remains close to my heart, and I dedicate time to working with an NGO, sharing the gift of learning with others. When I'm not coding or mentoring, you'll likely find me swimming—my way of unwinding and staying energized.
As I continue to grow as a developer, I'm eager to take on new challenges, refine my skills, and contribute to meaningful projects. The best is yet to come, and I'm excited about the journey ahead!
